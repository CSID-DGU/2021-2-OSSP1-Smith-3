How to use Mr.Smith from Switzerland


‘Mr. Smith from Switzerland’ consists of two modes
- learning mode
- challenge mode.

Let's learn to use learning mode!
The sentence on the left of the page is an example sentence for you to practice.
You can also check the meaning on the bottom of the example sentence.

1) press the “연습하기” button to start practice
2) than pronounce the example sentence! Sentence you have pronounced will be printed on the screen
3) press “ 종료하기” button to end the pronunciation.
4) to check the accuracy of your pronunciation, press “정확도 확인” button

If you want to return the previous example sentence or move on to the next example sentence,
use the triangle button at the bottom of the page.

Now let’s take a look at challenge mode!
Challenge mode includes two categories, which is tongue twister and newly coined word.
You can choose the category on the navigator bar on the right side on the website.
The method of using challenge mode is same as learning mode.
So please check the usage of Learning mode.
In the newly coined word page , meaning of newly coined words are provided.
It will be a great opportunity for you to learn about new words used in Korea!

So far, we've learned to use ‘Mr. Smith from Switzerland’.
